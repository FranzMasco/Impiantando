Welcome to Impiantando!

The purpose of this service is to provide a web application in order to manage and promote sport centers.
If you are the administrator of a sport center you can contact us at impiantando@gmail.com, attach a certificate to demonstrate your ownership and we are going to provide you the credentials to access the adminsitrator area.
On the other hand, if you are simply a courious sporty visitor you can explore the registered sport senters and discover their facilities and courses. What is more, you can create and account in order to register yourself to the activites offered.


We hope you enjoy using our software product. Remember that your feedback is important to us.
